  • Identified high-demand products and demand concentration areas to support inventory optimization and operational planning.
  • Delivered actionable recommendations through dashboard-driven insights, framed for executive-level consumption.

IBM Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

Customer retention is cheaper than acquisition. This project used IBM's telecom dataset to identify which customers were most at risk of churning — and why.

  • Analyzed 7,043 customer records to identify churn behavior and retention opportunities through pattern detection and segmentation.
  • Performed data cleaning, segmentation, and trend analysis using Excel functions and Pivot Tables to isolate high-risk cohorts.
  • Identified key churn drivers based on customer engagement, tenure, and service usage patterns.
  • Created management-ready reports highlighting churn risk segments with clear retention recommendations framed for action.
